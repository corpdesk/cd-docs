Getting Started with ASDAP

To get started with ASDAP, follow these simple steps to complete your registration and login process.

Step 1: Navigate to the ASDAP Website

Open your browser and go to https://asdap.net

Click on the Register button to navigate to the registration page.

Step 2: Registration Page
  • Note: Ensure your email is valid and accessible.
  • Choose a readable and recognizable username that your peers can easily identify you with.
  • Create a unique and secure password to protect your account.
Step 3: Verify Your Email

Check your inbox for an email from ASDAP with a verification link to complete your registration. Click the link to validate your email address.

The link contains a unique verification key that confirms your account.

Step 4: Sign In

Return to the ASDAP homepage and click on the Sign In button to navigate to the login page.
